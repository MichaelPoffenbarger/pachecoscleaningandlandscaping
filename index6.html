Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I have my lawn mowed?

Mowing Frequency

The frequency of lawn mowing depends on several factors:

  • Grass type (cool-season vs. warm-season)
  • Growing season (spring/summer vs. fall/winter)
  • Rainfall and irrigation
  • Desired lawn appearance

General Guidelines

  • Growing season: Once a week
  • Peak growing periods: Twice a week
  • Dormant seasons: Every 2-3 weeks
  • Follow the "one-third rule": Never remove more than 1/3 of the grass blade length in a single mowing

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I have my lawn mowed?

The frequency of lawn mowing depends on the season and grass type. Generally, during the growing season (spring and summer), once a week is recommended. In fall and winter, every 2-3 weeks may be sufficient.

What's included in a standard home cleaning service?

Our standard home cleaning service includes dusting, vacuuming, mopping, bathroom cleaning, kitchen cleaning, and general tidying. We can customize the service based on your specific needs.

How long does a landscaping design project typically take?

The duration of a landscaping design project varies depending on the size and complexity of the project. Small projects may take 2-4 weeks, while larger projects can take several months from design to implementation.

Are your cleaning products eco-friendly?

Yes, we use eco-friendly cleaning products that are safe for your family and pets. If you have specific preferences or sensitivities, please let us know, and we'll accommodate your needs.
